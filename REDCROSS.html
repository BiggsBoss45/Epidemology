Building a Center of Resilience

This Is More Than Bricks and Mortar—It’s a Promise

The new Red Cross Regional Headquarters in Austin is more than a building—it's a promise to strengthen our blood supply, empower volunteers, and meet every challenge with compassion and courage. For the first time, the facility will include a blood collection center, dramatically expanding our ability to deliver lifesaving blood products across Texas. Every gift brings us closer to unlocking a $1.37 million challenge grant from The J.E. and L.E. Mabee Foundation—but remaining funds must be raised by June 2026.
